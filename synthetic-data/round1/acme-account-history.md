## House Overview

House Acme Keep is a mid-sized trading house of 2,200 sworn members, seated in a fortified keep along the Kingsroad between King's Landing and Winterfell. They are the dominant supplier of bladed weapons and cured wolf pelts across the Seven Kingdoms. Their supply chain stretches from the Wolfswood in the North to the armorers of Lannisport in the West.

They adopted our Grand Ledger platform (trade logistics and inventory tracking) to replace a crumbling raven-and-scroll system that had caused three major shipment losses in 297 AC. The initial rollout was delayed by six moons due to a dispute between the Maester's Guild and the Head of Trade over who controlled the ledger. The system stabilised by late 299 AC and has been the backbone of their operation since.
