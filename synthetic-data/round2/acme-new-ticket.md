# House Acme Keep — New Support Ticket

## Ticket #1114 — Cross-Kingdom Shipping Optimizer: Northern Route Failure
**Opened:** Sixth Moon of 300 AC
**Raised by:** Marcus the Chain-Breaker
**Priority:** High
**Status:** Open — Escalated to CSM Jordan

**Summary:**
The Cross-Kingdom Shipping Optimizer, which was finally enabled on northern routes two moons ago, is routing weapon shipments through the Neck — a path that has been impassable since the Bolton occupation. Three weapon caravans were delayed by 11 days each, resulting in a breach of delivery terms with two of House Acme's largest weapon buyers (House Greyjoy and House Tully).

Marcus estimates the delays cost House Acme approximately 30,000 Gold Dragons in penalty clauses.

**Marcus's message (verbatim):**
> "This is a serious problem. We enabled the optimizer on your recommendation, and it has made things actively worse. The route data appears to be based on pre-war maps. We need this fixed before renewal or we will be seeking a credit against our contract."

**Root Cause (preliminary):**
The Shipping Optimizer's road network data has not been updated since 297 AC. It does not reflect closures caused by the War of the Five Kings. Our data team is working on a Westeros road network patch but it is not yet released.

**Outstanding Actions:**
- Jordan to issue a formal apology scroll and propose a service credit
- Data team to provide ETA on road network patch
- Consider whether to proactively disable the Optimizer for northern routes until the patch is live
