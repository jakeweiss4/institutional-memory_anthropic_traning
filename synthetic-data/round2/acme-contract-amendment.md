# House Acme Keep — Contract Amendment (300 AC)

**Amendment Date:** Fifth Moon of 300 AC
**Amendment Reference:** Grand Ledger Contract — Amendment I
**Signatory:** Maester Priya, Keeper of Contracts (acting authority — see leadership note)
**Original Contract Renewal Date:** Harvest Moon, 300 AC (unchanged)

## Changes to Contract Terms

### 1. Price Escalation Clause — Suspended
The 8% escalation clause that was to apply retroactively if the Wolf Pelt Grading Module was not deployed has been **waived** for this renewal cycle. House Acme successfully completed deployment of the grading module in the Fourth Moon of 300 AC (confirmed by Ticket #1108 resolution). The price hold negotiated by Lady Sarah stands.

### 2. Cross-Kingdom Shipping Optimizer — SLA Carve-Out Added
Following the northern route failures (see Ticket #1114), House Acme has requested a new SLA clause: if the Shipping Optimizer routes a caravan through a road closed for more than 30 days and causes a delivery breach, we owe a service credit equal to 50% of one month's ACV (20,000 Gold Dragons per incident, capped at two incidents per contract year).

**This clause was accepted by our legal team.** It has been added to the contract effective the Fifth Moon of 300 AC.

### 3. Siege Weapon Manifest Module — Added at Discount
Marcus's long-standing request (Ticket #1091) for a Siege Weapon Manifest module has been included in this amendment at a **30% introductory discount**: 28,000 Gold Dragons per year (standard price: 40,000). The discount expires at the following renewal.

**New ACV: 508,000 Gold Dragons** (up from 480,000).
