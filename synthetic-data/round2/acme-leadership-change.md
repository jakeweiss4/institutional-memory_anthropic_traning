# House Acme Keep — Leadership Change Notice

**Date:** Fifth Moon of 300 AC
**Source:** Official raven from Maester Priya, confirmed by Marcus the Chain-Breaker

## Lady Sarah of House Acme — Departed

**Lady Sarah is no longer Master of Coin at House Acme Keep.**

She departed in the Fourth Moon of 300 AC to accept a position as Royal Treasurer to Queen Cersei at King's Landing following the death of Tywin Lannister. Her departure was abrupt — she gave two weeks' notice and did not transition any vendor relationships directly.

Sarah was our executive sponsor and the original champion of the Grand Ledger platform. She is no longer a valid point of contact at House Acme.

**Do NOT direct renewal proposals or strategic conversations to Lady Sarah.** She has no authority over House Acme's contracts and has moved to a competing institution.

## New Master of Coin — Lord Edmyn Tully

**Lord Edmyn Tully** has been appointed Master of Coin effective the Fifth Moon of 300 AC. He is a cousin to House Tully of Riverrun, brought in for his reputation in trade law.

Lord Edmyn is new to the role and has no prior relationship with our platform. He is reviewing all vendor contracts as part of a broader treasury audit. He has not yet been briefed on the Grand Ledger.

**CSM Jordan has not yet established a relationship with Lord Edmyn.** This is an urgent priority before renewal.

Key facts about Lord Edmyn's posture:
- He is focused on cost reduction following the War of the Five Kings treasury losses
- He is skeptical of platforms he did not select himself
- Maester Priya is his trusted advisor and has been helpful in bridging the relationship

## Marcus the Chain-Breaker — Elevated

With the leadership transition, **Marcus the Chain-Breaker has taken on greater influence** in platform decisions. He was the internal champion for the Siege Weapon Manifest module expansion and has been vocal about the Shipping Optimizer failure. His relationship with Jordan is positive but he is frustrated about Ticket #1114.
